Features

Creative old tools

The familiar tools with incrediable looking

Cuties provides you the same tools as what other IDEs have: Code Editor, Compiler, and Debugger. You find out that they are totally different in Cuties, but much easier to use. The brand new user interface aimes to help you compose source code with joy. All the widgets are designed to keep you focused while you work. Now you can complete your stunning code within a brand new way.

Continue your work

Remain your thoughts, even every new

Cuties can remember what you did when you quit. When the next time you open your Cuties, you find that everything was still there. The place of the cursor, the place of the tab. Even the unsaved new files are also there. Cuties will save your creative status as much as possible, to continue your creative thoughts.
