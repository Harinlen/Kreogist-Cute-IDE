Features

Creative old tools

The familiar tools with incrediable looking

Cuties provides you the same tools as what other IDEs have: Code Editor, Compiler, and Debugger. You will get surprised because of the totally new easy-to-use appearances those traditional tools have. The brand new user interface aimes to help you write source code with joy. All the widgets are designed to keep you focused while you work, making your work more efficient.

Blossom your mind, everywhere

Start designing on your every device

Cuties lets you to compose your code effortlessly on your every devices. Because Cuties is a cross-platform application. You can get the same incredible experience wherever you are. Now you can complete your stunning code within a brand new way.

Simplicity

Powerful is now finally unified concise

When we are trying to build Cuties combines more and more features, we pay more attention to the way it will be used. We believes powerful is not the opposite of the concise. When you are coding, there is nothing but only editor on the display. When you want to compile or debug, the Docks will automatically appear. The way you use it is the same as what it looks like.

Continue your work

Remain your thoughts, even every new

Cuties can remember what you did when you quit. When the next time you open your Cuties, you will find that everything was still there. The place of the cursor, the place of the tab. Even the unsaved new files are also there. Cuties will save your creative status as much as possible, to continue your creative thoughts.
